About the Firm

Built on principle.
Driven by results.

Founded in 1994 by Margaret Calloway and David Park, our firm was established on a simple conviction: that every business deserves access to sophisticated legal counsel, delivered with integrity and a deep understanding of what drives growth.

Today, our team of eight attorneys and twenty-two professionals serves clients across central Ohio and beyond, from emerging enterprises to established market leaders.
