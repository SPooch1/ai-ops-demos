About Our Practice

Dentistry That Puts You First

Founded in 2010, Bright Line Dental has grown into one of Westerville's most trusted dental practices. Our team of three experienced dentists and dedicated support staff are committed to one thing: making every visit comfortable, efficient, and genuinely pleasant.

We've invested in the latest technology, from 3D cone beam imaging to digital smile design, because better tools mean better outcomes. But what truly sets us apart is how we treat people. You're never just a chart number here.
