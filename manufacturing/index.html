Our Story

Precision Is Not a Goal.
It's Our Standard.

Founded in 2008 by master machinist Dave Kowalski, Forge & Flow Manufacturing started with a single CNC mill and a commitment: every part leaves our floor at spec or it doesn't leave at all.

Today, our 32,000 sq. ft. facility in Columbus houses 14 CNC machines, a full welding bay, and a dedicated quality lab. We serve Tier 1 and Tier 2 suppliers in automotive and aerospace, delivering tight-tolerance components that keep critical systems running.

Our 45-person team brings an average of 12 years of shop floor experience. We're AS9100 certified and ITAR registered. When the margin for error is measured in microns, experience matters.
